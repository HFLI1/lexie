I am a Ph.D. candidate in Workforce Development and Education at The Ohio State University College of Education and Human Ecology. My research focuses on education policy, career and technical education (CTE), workforce development, education-to-workforce pathways, and educational equity.

I use mixed-methods research, policy evaluation, and quantitative analysis to study how education systems can better support students' access to career pathways and workforce opportunities. My current work includes research on CTE credentialing, English Learners' access to CTE pathways, and the role of education systems in supporting college-to-career transitions.

Before joining Ohio State, I completed my M.S.Ed. in Education Policy at the University of Pennsylvania Graduate School of Education and worked on research projects related to school policy, community-based interventions, and education data analysis.
